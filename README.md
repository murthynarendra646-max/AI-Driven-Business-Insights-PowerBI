# AI-Powered-Business-Insights-Dashboard

# Business Insights
The business has achieved total sales of 12.64M with a profit of 1.47M, resulting in a moderate profit margin (~11.61%), indicating scope for profitability improvement
Central region is the most profitable, significantly outperforming other regions, making it the key revenue and profit driver
Sales show a strong upward trend over time, indicating consistent business growth and increasing market demand
Technology category leads in overall sales, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies, highlighting product-level performance differences
Within sub-categories, Phones and Copiers generate the highest sales, contributing significantly to total revenue
Consumer segment dominates sales, while Corporate and Home Office segments contribute less, indicating uneven customer distribution
AI insights (Key Influencers) highlight that specific segments and regions disproportionately impact profit, rather than uniform contribution across all data points

# Recommendations
Focus expansion strategies on Central region, while identifying ways to improve underperforming regions
Increase investment in top-performing sub-categories like Phones and Copiers to maximize revenue
Diversify growth by strengthening Corporate and Home Office segments
Improve profit margins by optimizing costs and pricing strategies, as growth is currently volume-driven
Use AI-driven segmentation to prioritize high-value customers and regions
Develop targeted strategies for underperforming categories and regions to balance overall business performance
